About us

We are a spiritual family united in a covenant to desire and pursue a Trinitarian fullness for transformational impact to the glory of God. Trinitarian fullness is a word that helps us capture the three aspects of a balanced Christian life:

- Rooted in the Word of Christ

- Filled with the Power of the Holy Spirit

- Aflame with Passion for God the Father

Our vision

Our vision is to see a movement of churches both rural and urban whose members PROCLAIM and PORTRAY His glory, and POINT to His return for a perfect Kingdom.
